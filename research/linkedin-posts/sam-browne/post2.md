**Topic:** How to create a custom LinkedIn feed in 5 minutes

---

The LinkedIn feed is garbage now. Here's how to fix it in 5 minutes. There's a lot of junk on LinkedIn now. The newsfeed is a mess. But it's ok. You can just create your own. Here's how: 1. Search any keyword in the search bar. 2. Remove the keyword from the URL so you're left with linkedin.com/search/results/all/ 3. Select "Posts" and "Latest". Now you're seeing all the latest posts on LinkedIn. 4. Select "From Member" and add the creators you want to follow one by one. 5. Save the page as a bookmark. This is your custom feed.
