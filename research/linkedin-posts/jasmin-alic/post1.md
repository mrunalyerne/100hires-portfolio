**Topic:** 5 things Jasmin does on every LinkedIn post

---

Here's 5 things I do on every LinkedIn post. I haven't skipped some in 4+ years: 1. Write an ALT description for images: This is for SEO. When you "edit" any photo you add to a LinkedIn post, you can "describe it for Google". These days, LLMs read it, so your posts end up in Google search too. 2. Repost your own post: To reach new time zones and new audiences who haven't seen the post yet. I typically do this 6-8 hours after posting. After 1-2 days, simple delete the repost to avoid duplicate content. 3. Stay for 1 full hour to comment: I have the strongest community on planet Earth for a reason. And I consistently get 1,000+ engagements on every post. It's because I've never-once "posted and ghosted". 4. Add pinned comments to "nurture" your audience: Adding self-comments or bonus points under your posts brings more engagement, but more importantly, it builds a sense of "belonging" for everyone. 5. Use my "Dear son" method. Meaning: I start each post with "Dear son" and end with "Love, Dad". When I'm done writing, I simply delete those two parts. I did it in today's post too. That's why it reads so easily. For me, LinkedIn posting isn't just writing. There's a lot more that goes into it.
