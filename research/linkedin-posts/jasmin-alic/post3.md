**Topic:** LinkedIn growth — consistency, collaboration and community

---

LinkedIn gave you a platform to impact people's lives. Beyond just business. So let me say this: Double down on "what always works". 1. If you're just getting started, never quit. Keep posting relentlessly. Keep building endlessly. 2. If you're not getting leads consistently, get in front of more people. Whether via the comments, via DMs, or via live events. Collaborate. Fail. Talk. Sell. Do it all. Get your name out there. No one else will. 3. Find your tribe in the process. Support others and you'll be supported. It's the exact reason I built my Link Up community, so you have a place to "belong". This journey may be lonely. But the top isn't. Let's all strive to the top together.
