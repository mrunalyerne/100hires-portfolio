**Topic:** Why AI can't fix your LinkedIn writing without writing principles

---

Claude won't fix your LinkedIn post's "flow" unless: You know what a good "flow" is. Yeah, you can take your post and chuck it into Claude and ask it to "make my post flow better". But wtf does that even mean? Do you know? Does Claude know? Claude is trained to write based on whatever the average is unless you instruct it otherwise. So unless you: 1. Know what good writing looks like 2. Know what BAD writing looks like 3. Instruct your Claude to write to your rules You'll never get anything good out of Claude. This might feel fast, easy and fun to write your LinkedIn post like this but there's nothing fun about wasting your time with this stuff and getting no results from it. And that will happen, I promise you.
