**Topic:** AI writing as impressions arbitrage on LinkedIn

---

The biggest impressions arbitrage on LinkedIn in the writing niche RIGHT NOW is: AI. My average impressions per post over the last 12 months: 13,450. My last two posts on AI writing: 61,162 (+166 link clicks) 21,706 (+74 link clicks). Impressions aren't everything. But crazy easy way to capitalise on what is top of mind of almost everyone who's writing anything. Don't just post AI writing systems though. > talk about your own audience's problems with AI writing > talk about how you would solve the problem instead > tie it back to your core message or position. e.g. I know my audience use Claude to help format their LinkedIn posts. But I know Claude will default to formatting in short lines and mobile optimised if prompted lazily. So I wrote a post about why this is bad and how i would think about it instead, in relation to my core message: making linkedin writing fast, easy and fun. I'm not gonna go teaching AI writing all of a sudden or become an "AI expert". But I will be writing about: > where my audience go wrong with AI writing > how I use tools like MagicPost in my writing > why AI can make writing even more fast, easy and fun.
