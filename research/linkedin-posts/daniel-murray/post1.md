**Topic:** AI content quality gap — scaling without guardrails

AI didn't kill the editor. It just made them work harder. Most companies treat AI like a finish line. It's actually the starting gun. Markup AI surveyed marketers and brand leaders and found the uncomfortable truth: Teams are scaling AI content with zero guardrails on voice, tone, or messaging. 40% of C-suite thinks it's working beautifully. Only 26% of the marketers actually in the trenches agree. The people least involved are the most confident. Classic. The AI Trust Gap report names the disconnect and shows how to close it.
