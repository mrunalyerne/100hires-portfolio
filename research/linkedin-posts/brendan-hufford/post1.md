**Topic:** B2B buying cycle data — 272 days, 88 touchpoints

---

I've got some data that's gonna blow your mind: 272 days. That's how long it takes from the first ad impression to a closed-won deal according to Dreamdata. 9 MONTHS. NINE. On average, Dreamdata also sees 88 touchpoints across 4 channels for closed-won deals among their customers. Buyers are now 81% of the way through their buying process when they raise their hands. I'm so freaking tired of people thinking their company's sales cycle mirrors the marketing cycle. Capture demand, yes. But also do things that create demand for your product. Just make the investment, backed by data, knowing how long it actually takes. PS - All of this has gotten longer too. When I first shared this stuff 2 years ago, the marketing cycle was 192 days and buyers were only 57% of their way through. The buying cycle has gotten WAY more complicated.
