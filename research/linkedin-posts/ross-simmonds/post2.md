**Topic:** LLMs recommend brands with social proof receipts

---

LLMs don't recommend brands... They recommend brands with receipts. That means: • Fresh G2 & Capterra reviews • Active Reddit discussions • Educational YouTube videos • Third-party list mentions. Audit your last 90 days. What new proof have you published off your own site? If the answer is "none," start there.
