**Topic:** Answer Economy — B2B buyers now use AI instead of Google

---

For twenty years, buyers Googled. Got ten blue links. Built their own spreadsheets. The search engine pointed. They decided. That era is done. 51% now start their software research with an AI chatbot more often than Google. First impressions left your homepage. They now happen inside ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude. Often in rooms your brand isn't even in. If the AI can't find you, or misrepresents you, you're not in the consideration set. You don't exist. The brands winning AI citations are doing something boring. They've spent years executing fundamentals: ✅ Consistent review generation ✅ Third-party presence and community participation ✅ Content distributed across every relevant surface ✅ Clear, consistent positioning everywhere. Answer Engine Optimization is what happens when you execute the basics relentlessly for years.
