**Topic:** Best practices are statistically average — audience research is the edge

---

Most "best practices" are just statistically average decisions. They're what tends to work across many audiences, many contexts, and many companies. Literally average by definition. That's fine if your goal is to be competent and play it safe. But safe is forgettable. If you want your marketing to actually land, you need audience research. It's only audience research that can tell you what your audience resonates with, responds to, and eventually, amplifies.

---

**Why this post is valuable:**
Amanda challenges the "best practices" mentality — average tactics produce average results. For B2B SaaS LinkedIn content, audience research is the only way to create content that truly resonates and gets amplified.
