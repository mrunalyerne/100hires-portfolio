I could downplay getting into Forbes 30u30 last week. Truth is, I've wanted this more than anything. Every single day for the last 375 days, I wrote it down. I visualised it. I manifested it, and I did the work required. And the price to pay? Everything I willingly lost or did. The sacrifices and the late nights. The pressure vs the obsession. The belief vs having no proof. But if you ask me if all of it was worth it... I'll show you my parents' reaction. Those 50 seconds were worth the 5 years. To you, reading this. Thank you. I've read every comment. Every DM. This is a win for all of us. We did this. Together. A reminder from me: If you can't get a seat at the table, build your own one. With love, a girl who was told she'd never make it. PS: I wrote down my 1 goal every day on paper, I'd love to see yours. Comment it, and let's make it happen.
