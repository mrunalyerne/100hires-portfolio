**Topic:** LinkedIn algorithm 2026 — profile identity and content relevance

---

Most LinkedIn advice in 2026 is already outdated. Not because people stopped trying. Because LinkedIn changed the rules. I've helped +1,000 of companies with LinkedIn. And I keep seeing the same mistake. They invest in content. Hire someone to write posts. Get their team to auto-like and share. Meanwhile they skip the foundation. And in 2026, the foundation is everything. LinkedIn's new improved algorithm model no longer evaluates your post in isolation. It evaluates your entire professional identity in 3 integrated ways: Your profile, your posts, your engagement, basically your positioning. That means: → Posting frequently without clear added value is now actively hurting you → A brilliantly written post misaligned with your profile identity simply won't travel → Relevance to your niche outperforms volume. Every time.
